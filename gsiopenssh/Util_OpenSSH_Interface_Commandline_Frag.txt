== GSI-OpenSSH Commands ==

--
The link:#gsissh[gsissh], link:#gsiscp[gsiscp], and link:#gsisftp[gsiftp] commands provide
the same interfaces as the standard OpenSSH **ssh**, **scp**, and
**sftp** commands, respectively, with the added ability to perform X.509
proxy credential authentication and delegation.
